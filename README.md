# English

## Overview
**MoniPortal** is a lightweight Windows desktop utility designed for users with multi-monitor setups. It provides a compact, always-on-top control panel that displays live previews of your extended physical monitors and allows you to control them interactively by clicking directly on the previews. 

It is the perfect solution when your extended screens are out of your direct line of sight (e.g., rotated for presentations, digital signage, child monitoring, or mounted high up on a wall).

---

## Key Features

### 1. Live Preview & Interactive Control
* **Real-time Monitoring**: Monitor all your active extended screens through compact, responsive preview tiles.
* **Click & Scroll Forwarding**: Perform clicks, double-clicks, right-clicks, drags, and scroll-wheel actions directly on the preview window. MoniPortal automatically translates and forwards the mouse events to the absolute coordinates of the physical screen.

### 2. Global Hotkey Cursor Parking (Pause / F9)
* Instantly recall your physical cursor back to the center of the MoniPortal control panel from any distant monitor by pressing **`Pause`** or **`F9`** on your keyboard (works globally even when another application has focus).

### 3. Drag-and-Drop Window Dispatch (Pro)
* Move any open application window to a target monitor instantly by simply dragging it from the active window list drawer and dropping it onto the corresponding monitor's preview tile. No more tedious manual dragging across multiple screens.

### 4. Single-Screen Blackout / Privacy Dimming (Pro)
* Need privacy or want to save power on a specific screen? Turn any extended monitor completely black with a single click, overlaying a secure, frameless black mask while keeping background processes running.

### 5. Privacy First & Offline Friendly
* **100% Local**: No screen captures or cursor coordinates are uploaded. MoniPortal operates fully locally.
* **Offline Grace Period**: Licensing checks (via LemonSqueezy) only connect once on activation and re-verify periodically in the background, allowing complete offline usage for up to 14 days.

# 繁體中文

## 軟體簡介
**MoniPortal** 是一款專為多螢幕使用者設計的輕量級 Windows 桌面實用工具。它在您的主螢幕上提供一個置頂的精簡控制面板，顯示其他延伸實體螢幕的即時畫面，並讓您直接在預覽圖上點擊來進行反向操控。

當您的延伸螢幕不在您的直接視線範圍內（例如：轉向給客戶/學生的展示螢幕、展場數位看板、給小孩看影片的副螢幕、或是掛在高處的監控螢幕）時，MoniPortal 是最完美的解決方案。

---

## 核心功能

### 1. 即時畫面預覽與滑鼠穿透操作
* **即時監控**：在小巧的預覽視窗中同時監看所有延伸螢幕的動態。
* **滑鼠穿透映射**：直接在預覽畫面上進行點擊、雙擊、右鍵、拖曳與滾動滾輪，系統會自動將座標轉換並將滑鼠事件發送到目標螢幕上。

### 2. 全域滑鼠一鍵召回 (Pause / F9)
* 操作完遠端螢幕後，無需費力將滑鼠慢慢滑回來。只需按下鍵盤上的 **`Pause`** 或 **`F9`** 熱鍵（即使正在使用其他視窗也有效），游標就會立刻飛回 MoniPortal 控制面板中心。

### 3. 拖放丟視窗 ── 視窗分派 (Pro)
* 想要把某個視窗搬到另一個螢幕？展開視窗清單抽屜，直接用滑鼠按住該視窗項目，拖曳並「丟進」目標螢幕的預覽圖中，視窗就會立刻瞬移過去並最大化。

### 4. 延伸螢幕一鍵黑屏防窺 (Pro)
* 當某個螢幕需要隱私保護或暫時不使用時，點擊預覽圖上的「💡」按鈕，該實體螢幕會立刻蓋上一層全螢幕的純黑遮罩（不影響後台程式運行），達到防窺與省電效果。

### 5. 隱私至上與友善的離線機制
* **純本機運作**：畫面擷取與滑鼠軌跡絕不上傳，100% 保障您的數位隱私。
* **離線寬限期**：授權驗證（透過 LemonSqueezy）僅在首次啟用時連網，隨後提供 14 天的離線寬限期，無網環境也能放心使用。

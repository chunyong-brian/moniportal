*   **視窗丟過去就不見**：要把某個軟體移到其他螢幕，總是拖出邊界後就找不到了，滑鼠游標也跟著迷路。
*   **平板閒置很浪費**：手邊有一台舊 iPad 或 Android 平板，想當副螢幕卻嫌買實體線太貴、裝其他軟體太繁瑣。

**MoniPortal 是您的完美解答！** 它在主螢幕上提供一個精巧的「即時預覽面板」，讓您直接在預覽圖上點擊操控，甚至能「掃碼一秒把舊平板變成延伸螢幕」，100% 避免安裝任何 App 的資安與麻煩。

## ⚡ 核心功能

### 1. 即時畫面預覽與滑鼠穿透操作
在小巧的預覽圖裡即時監看其他螢幕，並**直接在預覽上**點擊、雙擊、右鍵、拖曳、滾動——系統自動換算座標，把滑鼠事件精準送到目標螢幕。

### 2. 全域一鍵召回游標 — `Pause` / `F9`
操作完遠端螢幕，一鍵把游標召回主螢幕的控制面板中心，即使焦點此時在其他程式也有效。

### 3. 拖放丟視窗 · *Pro*
從清單拖出任一視窗、丟進某螢幕的預覽圖，視窗立刻瞬移過去並最大化。不用再對著看不到的螢幕硬拖。

### 4. 一鍵螢幕黑屏防窺 · *Pro*
一鍵把某延伸螢幕蓋上全黑遮罩，達到防窺與省電，背景程式照常運行。

### 5. 區網投放到另一台電腦 · *Pro*
把您的某個螢幕投到另一台跑 MoniPortal 的電腦——區網內 **H.264** 低延遲視訊，用一次性 **PIN 碼**保護；對方只是「出借」一台螢幕當顯示面。

### 6. 投放到任何瀏覽器 — 免安裝 · *Pro*
把舊 iPad、Android 平板、電視瀏覽器或閒置筆電變成您的副螢幕。接收端只要**掃 QR Code**（或開網址），**完全不用裝任何 App**。頻寬極度優化（只送有變動的畫面），弱 Wi-Fi 也順，並使用每次連線的動態安全性權杖（Token）嚴格把關。

### 7. 隱私至上與離線使用
畫面處理 **100% 在本機**，絕不上傳。唯一的連網是啟用時的授權驗證，之後最長 **14 天**離線寬限期內都不需連網。

*   **Out of Sight, Out of Control**: Your extended screen is rotated for a client, mounted high, or in another room, making mouse control blind and frustrating.
*   **Lost Windows & Cursor**: Dragging a window to an unseen monitor feels like throwing it into a black hole—only to lose your cursor with it.
*   **Wasted Tablets**: You have a spare iPad or Android tablet, but buying expensive display cables or installing clunky client apps is too much hassle.

**MoniPortal solves this instantly!** It brings live previews of all your extended screens right to your main desktop, letting you interact with them directly, and **shares any screen to any browser via a simple QR code—no app install required.**

## ⚡ Key Features

### 1. Live Preview & Interactive Control
Watch your extended screens in compact preview tiles, and click, double-click, right-click, drag, and scroll **directly on the preview** — MoniPortal forwards the mouse events to the real screen's coordinates.

### 2. Global Hotkey Cursor Parking — `Pause` / `F9`
Snap your cursor back to the control panel from any distant monitor with one keypress, even when another app has focus.

### 3. Drag-and-Drop Window Dispatch · *Pro*
Drag any open window from the list and drop it onto a monitor's preview — it jumps there and maximizes. No more dragging across screens you can't see.

### 4. One-Click Privacy Blackout · *Pro*
Cover any extended monitor with a full black mask in one click — privacy and power-saving, while background apps keep running.

### 5. Cast to Another PC over your LAN · *Pro*
Send one of your screens to another PC running MoniPortal — low-latency **H.264** video over the local network, secured by a one-time **PIN**. The other PC just "lends" a monitor as the display surface.

### 6. Cast to Any Browser — Zero Install · *Pro*
Turn an old iPad, Android tablet, smart-TV browser, or spare laptop into an extra display. The receiving device just **scans a QR code** (or opens a URL) — **no app to install**. Bandwidth-smart (only changed frames are sent) so it stays smooth even on weak Wi-Fi, and access is gated by a per-session token.

### 7. Privacy First & Offline Capable
Screen handling is **100% local** — nothing is uploaded. The only network use is a quick license check on activation, then it runs offline for up to **14 days** between checks.

## ❓ FAQ

#### Q1: Does MoniPortal upload my screen data?
**Absolutely not.** All screen capture, encoding, and transmission occur **100% locally within your device or local network (LAN)**. No screen data ever touches the cloud. The only internet connection made is a 1-second check during license activation.

#### Q2: Does the Web Receiver support my old iPad or tablet?
**Yes!** As long as your device has a modern web browser (Safari, Chrome, Firefox, etc.) and is connected to the same Wi-Fi network, any device can scan the QR code and display your screen without installing any software.

#### Q3: How many computers can I activate with a Pro License?
Each Pro license key allows **up to 3 devices** to be activated simultaneously. If you upgrade your PC, you can click "Deactivate" inside the app to free up a slot instantly.
